Music

Kathi Nordone inherited a love for music at an early age. Her Italian mother’s musical interests ranged from Luciano Pavarotti to Abba and Ray Charles. Her father fancied himself a kindred spirit to Hank Williams, Johnny Cash, and John Denver. These artists would leave a lasting impact on Kathi and inspire her to begin songwriting at thirteen years old. While at college, Mark Irwin, a then fledgling country songwriter, heard her singing and invited her to join his band. She later moved to Austin to pursue a career as a solo folk artist. Throughout her career she has worked with various songwriters and musicians including Charles Larkey, Leonard Cohen, Mark “Kaz” Kazanoff and Rick McRae. Kathi believes that “at the heart of a good song is a sort of silence where people can hear themselves think…I just want to give them the space to find their happiness.”
